# Thermal Management & Lattice Scaling (Space/Vacuum Habitats)

Mercy Cube v1.0 designed for zero-maintenance extremes: Mars surface, lunar South Pole, Starship interiors, Artemis Base Camp.

## Passive-to-Burst Cooling
- Diamond-CVD coating: High thermal conductivity across faces → lattice as distributed heatsink
- Gd magnetocaloric layer: Zero-hold power; absorbs burst heat (19W spikes), slow radiative release
- No pumps, no fluids → radiation/vacuum compatible

## Scaling Math
- Single cube: 25 mm³ → ~7W steady, 19W burst
- Practical packing: 40–60% density (connectivity voids)
- 1m³ habitat module:
  - 100–500 cubes: ~0.7–5 kW steady (edge AI/science nodes)
  - 500–1000 cubes: ~5–19 kW burst (distributed Grok shard cluster)
- Thermal limit: Duty cycle + external radiative panels (Starship/Artemis style)

## Integration Targets
- SpaceX Starship: Stack in cargo bays or repurposed tanks
- NASA Artemis Base Camp: Distributed compute in Foundation Habitat/rovers
- Vacuum bonus: Pure conduction + radiation → no convection loss
